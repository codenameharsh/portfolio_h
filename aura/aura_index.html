Aura

Brand Identity Design Brand Style Guide Logo Design Fashion Illustration

PROJECT OVERVIEW

AURA is a conceptual luxury fashion brand created around a simple idea: real luxury isn't about what something costs, it's about the glow you carry when you wear it. I wanted to see if I could translate celestial inspiration into something refined and emotionally real, without it feeling like a costume party.

Deliverables:

What came out of it was a 19-page brand blueprint covering everything from visual identity to packaging to how the digital experience should feel.

THE CHALLENGE

Defining Celestial Luxury

Most luxury fashion feels untouchable—cold, distant, almost transactional. I wanted to create something that felt mysterious and luminous, but still grounded. Something that could sit comfortably next to heritage brands without losing its warmth.

THE PROBLEM

How do you design something that feels weightless but also belongs in high fashion?

The tricky part was walking the line between magic and modernity—keeping the dreaminess without veering into crystal shop territory.

I dug into what the cosmos means to people emotionally, and one thing kept coming up: stars and light aren't just pretty—they're tied to identity, aspiration, who we are when no one's looking.

KEY INSIGHT

Luxury isn't just a price point—it's the glow of an experience.

That became the heart of AURA: a brand that reflects the personal halo of light you already have.

IDENTITY & CORE VALUES

STARBORN IDENTITY

Where Dreams Weave Light

To keep everything anchored, I built out what I called a Cosmic Core, rooted in five core values:

  • Artistry — intentional, handcrafted design
  • Emotion — fashion as feeling, not spectacle
  • Elegance — quiet confidence over excess
  • Imagination — dreamlike, but restrained
  • Authenticity — beauty that feels personal

The Refinement Arc

My early ideas were way too on-the-nose. Taglines like "Fashion from the Stars" sounded more decorative than meaningful.

THE BREAKTHROUGH

I realized I needed to focus on the act of creation, not just the celestial imagery. The final tagline, "Where Dreams Weave Light," reframed luxury as dreams becoming form.

THE ASTRAL COMPASS

Voice & Personality

To figure out how AURA should sound, I created The Astral Compass—a personality matrix right at the intersection of Refined and Poetic.

Voice Characteristics

  • Graceful, Soulful, Mythic
  • A Whispered Story Wrapped In Starlight

The Refinement Arc

Balancing casual and formal was harder than I thought. Traditional luxury rules felt stiff and didn't fit.

THE BREAKTHROUGH

I decided that being soulful and mythic mattered more. The voice became intimate instead of distant.
